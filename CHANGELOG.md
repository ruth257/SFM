### Core Concepts
- **Pseudo-Antonym**: Terms appearing interchangeable but representing mutually exclusive strategies
- **Semantic Fork**: Decision point requiring user input before optimization
- **Fulcrum Token**: Low-resolution term needing precision refinement
- **Cohesion by Constraint**: Pre-filtering alternatives for semantic alignment
